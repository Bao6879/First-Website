Quotes that I like

Although I know some people live by the quotes that they like, I cannot say the same to myself. I try to adhere to them as much as I can, but emotional instability or other sudden events can still disrupt it. Still, if you were my friend, you would see me quoting these quite a bit.

Without further ado, these are 7 quotes that I like in no particular order:

  • Not much in life really matters:
    • What is life, but death pending?

    - Vampyr -

    My personal interpretation of the quote is that the bad things in life do not matter at all, since death is waiting at the end anyway, so there is not much of a point feeling too down about something.

    Obviously, you can make the argument that the quote means the other thing as well, where good things do not matter either, but I have remedied it with the quotes below.

    This is only a short part of the full quote: here. I like this quote not only because it sounds very cool, but also because it enables me to think more positively about life.

  • Never give up
    • It's not over till it's over.

    Unknown

    A classic quote that I am not sure of the origin, it is as simple as it sounds. Another variation of this could be "As long as you're breathing, it's winnable", which is a classic Vietnamese quote, especially game. It is also one of the quotes I cannot live by 100%, as when you really just lost, there is not much you can do, unless you are god.

  • Adaptability
    • Improvise, adapt, overcome.

    Thomas Highway from Heartbreak Ridge

    This quote enables me to continue changing, adapting to the surroundings when neccessary, mainly in new environment, e.g: Studying abroad. Also quite catchy, if I would say so myself.

  • Nothing is impossible
    • Nothing is hard
    Fear only the weak willed
    Moving mountains and filling oceans
    Where there's a will, there's a way

    Uncle Ho

    This is a quote back in the war days of Vietnam, spoken by the leader and liberator of Vietnam himself, uncle Ho. Unfortunately, my translation cannot do it justice.

    The quote is quite simple in nature, similar to never give up and nothing is impossible. I mainly quote this line in despair filled scenarios, e.g: Blackjack, Poker.

  • There is no such thing as an early victory
    • Never celebrate too early

    Unknown

    In contrast to the positive ones above, here we have a quote telling us to exercise caution. This quote is invaluable in the games that I have played, mainly racing and rhythm games, where one mistake could cost the entire run. I also apply it to projects that I code as well, finishing it before hosting a celebration to avoid procrastination.

  • Motivation
    • Right. Let's get going then.

    Me :)

    This one was created and used mainly by me, similar to a mantra, forcing me to go do whatever I have left behind. It is a simple sentence, but I use it a lot, especially chasing deadlines.

  • Be cautious
    • Never underestimate anything.

    Unknown

    Another simple one to understand, yet it is vital in judging opponents in PvP games and tasks at hand. Following this quote, I started on my project early and was able to figure out a critical error within it that made me scrap the entire project. If I had left it till later, it would be catastrophic.
