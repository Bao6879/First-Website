The table below displays a short summary of my life. Unfortunately, since I cannot remember things too clearly from years ago, the dates and events might be slightly inaccurate. The table only lists events that I would consider major in my life. The cyan-colored rows are sub rows that are events that occured during the period of time of an event above.

Start End Event Notes
Aug 31, 2006 Aug 31, 2006 Birthdate I was born :D
2006 2009 Did not live in Hanoi During this time, my family's economic situation was not optimal, so I lived in the countryside with my grandparents. This positively affected life later on, mainly relationship with people from the town.
2009 2009 Began learning English My mother placed a high priority on my English skills. As such, I started at a much younger age than my peers. This benefited my life massively later on, as you shall see.
2011 2011 Began learning Math As all Asians do, I started learning Math at a reasonably young age. This gave me a slight edge over foreign competitors in this part.
2012 2017 Primary school I went to a pretty good primary school near my house. The education system in Vietnam is different from Ireland, where Primary school stretches from 6 years old to 10 or 11. I was quite good at Math and English starting 7 years old, regularly competing in district-wide competitions. It was the golden age, as my mother would call it.
2017 2017 First city-wide competition The competition was in English. Sadly, with my weird studying method (intuitively instead of by the book), I only got a bronze prize.
2017 2021 Entered Secondary school Secondary school in Vietnam stretches from 11 years old to 14 or 15. I went to a very average school, and my academic abilities tanked as a result of finding no competition. However, I would say it is the most fun out of all compulsory education.
Late 2019 Early 2020 The Dark Ages This was a lengthy period when I was severely addicted to video games. I will not go into too much detail here, but it was quite devastating to my academic and physical abilities.
Early 2020 2021 Preparing for entrance exams The high school entrance exam is similar to the leaving cert in Ireland. However, what I started preparing for is the specialized tests for gifted schools, orders of magnitudes more difficult than the normal version. I went to a lot of different teachers to learn 1 subject: Math.
March 18, 2021 March 2021 The end of the Dark Ages A decimating rejection snapped me out of the dangerous mental state I was in, and I started to focus on studying again, harder than ever before. This played a key role in my behavior later on, even now.
May 2021 May 2021 High school entrance exams 4 years of secondary school culminated in this event. I annihilated the ordinary level tests. Can you guess what happened with the specialized one?
Sep 2021 May 2024 HSGS HSGS stands for High School for Gifted Students. With slight bias, I would say it is the best High school (academically) in Hanoi. Objectively speaking, we dominate the international stage in Vietnam, and we are very proud of that. Most of the knowledge I have now comes from here.
Oct 2021 Oct 2022 Pre Olympiad for Informatics A very fun and informative class, led by the most brilliant Informatics teacher HSGS has ever seen, I can attribute 99% of my IT knowledge to this class. Its purpose was to select students to participate in the National Olympiad. I failed this test, somewhat intentionally, placing 16th out of 50. Having guaranteed a seat in one of the best IT Universities in Vietnam, my eyes drifted to open seas.
Jan 2023 May 2023 Preparing for the SAT The first step for my portfolio is a good SAT score. 1350 in the first practice test told me I needed to prepare though, and the test took place in May. My strong English background helped a lot in the preparation, allowing me to focus on the trickiest parts of the English language: Advanced grammar and vocabulary.
May 5, 2023 May 5, 2023 The SAT 1550 for the first test, this was very good at the time, placing me firmly in the top 3 of the school at the time. Having conquered the SAT, I moved on to the TOEFL.
May 2023 July 2023 TOEFL preparation Unlike the SAT, which I studied by myself at Khan Academy, I studied under a teacher for the TOEFL.
July 22, 2023 July 22, 2023 The TOEFL 113/120, which is just barely less than my wish of 115. But it was fine, so I focused on schoolwork more. That is, until the next event.
Aug 2023 May 2024 Research Having acquired good scores for studying abroad, me and my family began researching to see what country to go to. Sometimes I wonder what would have happened if I chose Japan instead, but the language barrier was quite intimidating.
Aug 2023 Dec 2023 2nd SAT preparation Aiming for the max score of 1600, I studied the SAT harder than ever before. It could be considered a Dark Age for me, it makes me shudder whenever I remember it. This was also where most of the quotes came from. You can check them out here.
Dec 2, 2023 Dec 2, 2023 2nd SAT I fell ill just the day before, leading me to enter the examination with 3 hours of sleep. Was it that? Was it due to insufficient preparations? The 1560 prompted self-doubt in my mind. Thankfully, the reason I did not fall into depression and despair would be friends and games.
Dec 2023 May 2024 Final semester of High School The exams have removed their safeguard, leading schoolwork to grow exponentially in difficulty. Luckily, I passed the final exam with decent marks.
Sep 3, 2024 Sep 3, 2024 Depart from Vietnam Like sailing an unknown sea, it was exciting and scary at the same time. Still, I can confidently say it was worth it.
Sep 6, 2024 Now Entered SETU And now here I am, coding the website assignment. I wonder if I had ever thought I would get this far back in the Dark Ages.
